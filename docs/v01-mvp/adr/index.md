## Decision Principles

In making the above decisions, we follow these principles:

1. **Data-Driven**: Decisions based on data and facts
2. **Progressive**: Support progressive development and iteration
3. **Open Standards**: Prefer open protocols and interfaces
4. **User-Centric**: Prioritize user needs and experience
5. **Technical Feasibility**: Ensure solutions are technically feasible
6. **Maintainability**: Consider long-term maintenance costs
